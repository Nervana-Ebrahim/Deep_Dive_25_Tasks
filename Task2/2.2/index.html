HOW TO USE

________

  1. Apply to clean, moisturized skin, focusing
    on your pulse points, including your
    wrists, neck, behind the ears, folds of your
    elbows, and behind the knees.

  2. Avoid rubbing into skin to maintain the integrity of
    the fragrance.

  3. For extra longevity, apply an unscented
    lotion to pulse points before applying.

  4. Have fun with layering! The possibilities
    are endless when you layer scents. Mix,
    match, and create a custom scent just for
    you, perfect for any mood or occasion.
